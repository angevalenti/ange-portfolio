Pro-Tip: The SIFT Method

S

Stop

Feel a strong emotion? Pause. Don't share until you verify.

I

Investigate the Source

Who wrote this? What is their reputation or expertise?

F

Find Better Coverage

Look for other trusted sources reporting the same thing.

T

Trace Claims

Go back to the original context or research paper.
