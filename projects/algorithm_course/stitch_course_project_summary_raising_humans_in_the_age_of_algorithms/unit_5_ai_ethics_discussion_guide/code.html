01. Conversation Starters

1

Deepfakes & Digital Truth

"If you saw a video of a friend saying something mean, but their voice sounded slightly 'off,' would you believe it? How do we verify truth when seeing is no longer believing?"

2

Academic Integrity vs. Assistance

"Where is the line between using AI as a tutor and using it to do the work for you? If AI writes the outline but you write the words, is it still your original thought?"

3

Algorithmic Bias

"AI learns from the past, which includes human prejudices. If an AI helps choose who gets a job or a loan, how do we ensure it isn't repeating our old mistakes?"

4

Emotional Replacement

"If an AI chatbot is always nice to you and never gets tired of listening, is it a better 'friend' than a human? What is missing from a connection that has no heartbeat?"

5

The Privacy Trade-off

"We give AI our data so it can be more helpful. How much of our personal thoughts or private family moments are we willing to trade for convenience?"
