Unit 1: Digital Wellness

Smooth Transitions: Ending Screen Time Without the Meltdown

A research-backed framework designed to help parents navigate the toughest part of the digital day. This printable guide provides the exact steps to bridge the gap between pixels and reality.

auto_awesome_motion

The 3-Step Framework

1

The Warning

Set expectations with clear, time-bound alerts. Instead of shouting from another room, get on their eye level. Use "When/Then" statements: "When this episode is over, then we are going to set the table."

2

The Bridge

Create a mental shift from digital to physical. Ask a question about what they just watched: "Who was your favorite character in that level?" This pulls them out of the "flow state" gently before the device is turned off.

3

The New Activity

Immediate engagement in a high-interest task. Don't leave a vacuum. Have the next activity ready to go—whether it's a snack, a favorite toy, or a physical challenge like "race you to the kitchen!"
