Middle School Tech Contract

A sophisticated collaborative framework for fostering healthy digital habits and mutual trust between students and guardians.

person

Student Commitments

Privacy & Security

I will protect my personal identity, never share passwords with peers, and keep sensitive family information offline.

Digital Etiquette

I will practice empathy in all digital spaces, avoiding cyberbullying, gossip, or any behavior that compromises my integrity or others' wellbeing.

Life Balance

I will prioritize sleep, physical activity, and face-to-face social interactions over screen-based entertainment.

supervisor_account

Parent Commitments

Empathetic Support

I will provide a safe space for you to report digital mistakes or concerns without immediate judgment or fear of device removal.

Active Modeling

I will lead by example, adhering to "device-free zones" (like dinner) and managing my own digital distractions in your presence.

Privacy Respect

I will respect your growing need for digital independence and will only monitor activity if safety concerns arise, with clear communication.

gavel

Mutually Agreed Consequences

Define what happens if the terms above are not met. Focus on restorative actions and logical consequences...
