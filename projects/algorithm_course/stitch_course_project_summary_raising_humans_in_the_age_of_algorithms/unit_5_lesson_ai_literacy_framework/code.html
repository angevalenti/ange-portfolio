Understanding the AI Literacy Framework

Moving beyond the hype: empowering your child to navigate the generative era with confidence and wisdom.

01 How Generative AI Works

At its core, Generative AI (like ChatGPT or Midjourney) uses Large Language Models (LLMs). Think of an LLM as a highly sophisticated autocomplete system that has read almost everything on the public internet. It doesn't "know" facts; it predicts the next most likely word in a sequence based on patterns.

The 3 Pillars of AI Literacy for Kids

fact_check

Critical Consumption

Questioning the output, checking for bias, and identifying "hallucinations" (AI mistakes).

brush

Creative Co-creation

Using AI as a partner to brainstorm, draft, and refine original human ideas.

gavel

Ethical Agency

Understanding privacy, academic integrity, and the human impact of AI use.

Practical Tips: Prompting with your child

lightbulb

The "Role-Task-Format" Method

Encourage your child to prompt like this: "You are a [Role]. Please [Task] in the style of [Format]."

"You are a biology teacher. Explain how photosynthesis works for an 8-year-old using a superhero metaphor."
