The 4 Pillars of a Family Agreement

A balanced agreement focuses on four key dimensions of digital life. These pillars form the foundation of your healthy tech household.

home_pin

Time & Place

Defining when and where devices are allowed. Establishing "Tech-Free Zones" like the dining table or bedrooms after 9 PM helps preserve face-to-face connection.

  • check_circle No-phone dinners
  • check_circle Central charging station
visibility_lock

Content & Privacy

What we share and what we watch. Aligning on age-appropriate apps, media ratings, and personal data boundaries to ensure digital safety and integrity.

  • check_circle Privacy setting audits
  • check_circle App approval process
favorite

Etiquette & Kindness

How we treat others online. Instilling the "Grandmother Rule": never post something you wouldn't want your grandmother (or future employer) to see.

  • check_circle Conflict resolution
  • check_circle Anti-bullying stance
balance

Consequences & Rewards

Fair boundaries and incentives. Agreements work best when they aren't just punitive. Build in "Tech Earned" rewards for responsible usage and consistent adherence.

  • check_circle Agreed-upon violations
  • check_circle Trust-building goals
lightbulb

Pro Tip: Modeling the Behavior

"Agreements fail when parents are exceptions to the rules. If dinner is tech-free, your phone stays in the other room too. Leadership starts with your own screen habits."
