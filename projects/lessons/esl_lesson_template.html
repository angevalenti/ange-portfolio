Hello, School!
Classroom & School Community
✦ All WIDA Levels · Entering → Bridging
🎯
Today's Learning Goal
We will learn the names of classroom objects, practice greetings, and share our names with a partner. By the end, you will be able to say: "My name is ___ and I see a ___ in our classroom."
📚
What We'll Learn
Classroom objects · Colors · Numbers 1–10 · Greetings · How to introduce yourself
🗣️
Language Functions
Greet others · Name and request objects · Follow 1–2 step directions · Introduce yourself
📋
PA Core Standards
CC.1.1.K.B · CC.1.1.K.C · CC.1.5.K.A · CC.1.5.K.D
⏱️
Time & Materials
~45 minutes · Headphones recommended for audio · Printables available at end
📝 Teacher Note
Before starting, activate prior knowledge by asking students: "What do you see in our classroom?" Allow home language responses and bridge to English using picture cards.
