Level 3 — Write 3 sentences about yourself
👇 Write 3 complete sentences. Tell us your name, where your family is from, and one tradition you have.
Use these frames if you need help:
My name is ___ and I am from ___.
In my family, we ___.
One tradition I have is ___.
Level 4 — Write a paragraph about your identity
👇 Write a paragraph (4–5 sentences) about who you are. Include: name, origin, family, a tradition, and how it makes you feel.
Academic language to try:
My identity includes… · My heritage is… · A tradition that is important to my family is… · This is significant because… · I feel proud when…
Level 5 — Write a compare & contrast paragraph
👇 Write a paragraph (5–6 sentences) that compares a tradition from your culture with another tradition you have learned about. Use transition words.
Transition words & academic frames:
Similarly… · In contrast… · Both cultures… · One difference is… · Despite this… · This reflects the idea that…
