Overview

Science that
actually sticks.

Study Buddies is a growing series of interactive science lessons designed to make complex body systems feel exciting and accessible for elementary learners. Each lesson is a single-file HTML experience — no frameworks, no dependencies — packed with animated SVG diagrams, drag-and-drop activities, interactive quizzes, and sound effects. The design evolves per lesson, giving each body system its own visual personality while maintaining a consistent level of interactivity and engagement.

Approach

Teach first.
Then play.

Every lesson follows a teach-first sequence: concept introduction, visual exploration, guided interaction, and assessment. Mnemonics, animations, and games aren't decorative — they're the mechanism of instruction. Each section earns its complexity only after the foundation is in place. The result feels more like an experience than a worksheet.

The Lessons

Lesson 01 · Circulatory System
The Heart & Blood Vessels

Heart, blood, and blood vessels — explore how your body's highway system works with interactive diagrams, drag-to-order activities, and a clickable 4-chamber heart.

  • 9 interactive sections
  • SVG cell diagram with 9 clickable organelles
  • Drag-to-order & drag-and-match quizzes
  • Animated 4-chamber heart diagram
  • Step-by-step blood flow animation
  • 8-question scored final quiz

Lesson 02 · Respiratory System
Lungs, Breath & Gas Exchange

Follow oxygen from your nose to your alveoli with animated journeys, a breathing exercise, gas exchange mini-games, and Web Audio sound effects throughout.

  • 8 interactive sections
  • Clickable SVG respiratory diagram (8 hotspots)
  • Animated 7-stop oxygen journey
  • Breathe Along exercise synced to sounds
  • Gas exchange molecule mini-game
  • 10-question scored final quiz
