Who I Am

I'm a certified K–12 educator with credentials in ESL, Art, and Special Education, and a background in graphic design. For years I worked in classrooms — developing curriculum, supporting diverse learners, and building instructional materials from scratch.

That foundation, combined with professional design experience at Penn Foster Group and All About Dance, Inc., puts me in a position most instructional designers don't have: I understand both sides of the table. The pedagogical rigor and the visual craft.
